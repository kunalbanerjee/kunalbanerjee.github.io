About

Hi! I am currently pursuing my PhD degree from the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kharagpur under the supervision of Prof. Chittaranjan Mandal and Prof. Dipankar Sarkar. My PhD thesis title is "Translation Validation of Optimizing Transformations of Programs using Equivalence Checking".

Research Interests

  • Translation Validation of Optimizing Transformations
  • Automatic Program Correction and Evaluation
  • Secure Hardware Design to Counter Power Analysis Attacks
